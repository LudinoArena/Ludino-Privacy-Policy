02   Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated January 2024

This policy explains how LUDINO LIVE and its affiliated companies (“LUDINO,” “we,” or “us”) process personal data collected through the LUDINO LIVE app, LUDINO LIVE LITE, ludino.live, and related services (collectively, the “LUDINO Services”).

i

Questions about how we use your personal data? Email feedback@ludino.live.

Information we collect

We collect information needed to provide and improve the LUDINO Services. This may include information you provide directly and information collected when you use our services.

Information you provide

  • Registration details, including date of birth, phone number or email, password, user identifier, and language.
  • Profile details such as alias, profile images, gender, hometown, self-introduction, and linked social media details.
  • User-generated content including comments, images, videos, messages, livestreams, and other uploaded materials.
  • Payment and billing information for transactions.
  • Location data when you grant permission.
  • Information from third-party accounts you choose to connect.

Information collected automatically

  • Browsing and search history and interactions with other users.
  • Device identifiers, IP address, mobile carrier, and operating system details.
  • Usage data, cookies, and similar technologies used for analytics and service improvements.
  • Mobile advertising identifiers used to support relevant advertising.
  • Metadata connected to user-generated content, such as hashtags and timestamps.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the LUDINO Services.
  • Manage accounts and provide customer and technical support.
  • Review content and enforce our policies.
  • Personalize content and advertisements based on activity.
  • Protect users, prevent fraud, and maintain service security.
  • Create aggregated, non-personally identifiable analytics and business insights.

How we share information

Your information may be shared with other users when your profile or activity is public; with service providers supporting payments, analytics, operations, and security; with authorities when required by law; and with affiliates or a buyer as part of a merger, acquisition, or similar business transaction.

International transfers and security

Your data may be stored and processed in countries whose data protection laws differ from those in your jurisdiction. We use safeguards intended to support compliance with applicable regulations. We also use measures such as encryption, authentication, and access controls to protect personal data; however, no online service can be completely secure.

Your choices and rights

You can use in-app settings to manage privacy preferences, location access, cookies, and certain data-processing choices. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your data; withdraw consent; or object to processing. Submit requests to feedback@ludino.live.

Children, retention, and updates

LUDINO LIVE does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we discover such data, we will take steps to delete it. We retain information while your account remains active and as otherwise required for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can request account deletion through app settings or by email.

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the LUDINO Services after an update means the revised policy applies. California residents should also understand that applicable privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act may supplement this policy.

Submitting a takedown notice

A copyright owner or authorized representative should provide:

  1. Legal name and complete contact information.
  2. A description of the copyrighted work and the copyright owner.
  3. A precise description or URL showing where the disputed content appears.

  5. A request to remove or disable access to the material.
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the notice is accurate and you are authorized to act.

After receiving a valid notice, we may remove or disable access to the material and notify the affected user in accordance with applicable law.

05   Authorities

Law Enforcement Request Policy

Updated February 2020

LUDINO LIVE responds to law-enforcement and other valid legal requests for user data in accordance with its terms, privacy policy, and applicable law.

Types of requests

01

Data requests

Except for emergencies, requests must be served through valid legal process. Depending on the information sought, this may include a subpoena, court order, warrant, Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty request, or another process recognized by applicable law.

02

Emergency requests

We may disclose limited information when we believe in good faith that delay could create a real and imminent danger of death, serious bodily harm, harm to a minor, or another emergency. Requests must come from a law-enforcement officer and be signed under penalty of perjury.

03

Preservation requests

For a valid request connected to an official investigation or proceeding, we may preserve existing user data for 30 days or a longer period while awaiting formal legal process, as permitted by law.

04

Other authorities

We consider lawful requests from regulators and tax authorities. Contact the LUDINO LIVE legal team at legal@ludino.tv.

Required request information

Requests must be on official agency letterhead and include the legal process, agency and agent names, badge or identification number, official email, direct telephone number, mailing address, requested response date, the specific user’s LUDINO LIVE ID or UID, and a precise description of the information requested.

We may reject requests that are incomplete, invalid, overly broad, vague, unclear, or unduly burdensome. If a request is not in English, an English translation is encouraged. We generally disclose no more information than legally necessary.

User notice and consent

We generally notify users before disclosing their information unless notice is prohibited by valid legal process or law, could create a risk of injury or death, or involves potential harm to minors. When a nondisclosure order expires or is overturned, we may notify the affected user. A consenting user should ordinarily be directed to obtain available account information through their own account.
